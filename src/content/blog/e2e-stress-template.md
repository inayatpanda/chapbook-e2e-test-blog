This how-to walks through publishing your first post with Chapbook, start to finish.

### What you'll need

A GitHub account, a repository for your blog, and about ten minutes.

### Steps

First, connect your repository in Settings and pick a branch.

Then write your post in blocks and hit Publish — Chapbook commits it for you.

### Tips

Keep drafts as draft:true until ready, and preview before you publish.
